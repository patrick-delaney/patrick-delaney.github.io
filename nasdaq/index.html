Client

In 2022, Nasdaq came to Postlight with a big question: How much could we improve their website in 12 months? Uhh, like Nasdaq.com? Like the whole site? Yep, the whole site.

Challenge

We had to start from scratch, canvas the entire Nasdaq.com website, pitch a focused track of work, and execute within a year.

Solution

12 months later, we proposed, planned, and executed an overhaul of Nasdaq’s fintech portfolio; Nasdaq Solutions. We introduced a flexible templating IA, custom navigation, and a modular design system that later expanded to the rest of the site.

Goals

  • Overhaul Solutions IA, UX, and brand without scrapping live site.
  • Increase conversion rate, drive the right customers to the right solutions.
  • Empower Nasdaq to craft experiences within their CMS.

Where to start?

Nasdaq's live portfolio was simple, but limited in visual language and content strategy.

With one template for dozens of acquisitions and multiple overlapping groups, portfolio items felt homogenous, indistinct, and buried.

A new strategy

The new Solutions ecosystem was optimized to champion Nasdaq's roster of talent while ensuring a cohesive, uniform visual language that tied everything together.

Mobile view

Content strategy

We sorted Nasdaq’s Solutions portfolio into templates focused on KPIs like Lead Volume, Lead Quality, and Relationship Building.

We organized these templates with an IA of nested microsites, creating a content ecosystem that was holistic but allowed for stakeholder independency.

Design system

We adopted an agile approach to tactically shipping pages that fed back into a dynamic, evolving design system.
