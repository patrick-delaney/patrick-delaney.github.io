Project goals

Nasdaq came to us with a big question

How much could we improve their website in 12 months? Uhh, like Nasdaq.com? The whole site? Yep, the whole site.

The challenge

Our team had to start from scratch, canvas the entire Nasdaq.com website, pitch a focused track of work, and execute within a year.

The solution

We proposed, scoped, and executed an overhaul of Nasdaq's fintech portfolio, Nasdaq Solutions. We introduced a new IA, navigation, and design system that later expanded to the entire site.

Big bold headline that denotes a new section

Content strategy

We sorted solutions by focusing on core KPIs like lead volume, lead quality, and relationship building.

Then we built templates around these KPIs, creating a new IA of nested microsites that presented a united front but allowed stakeholders to stand out.

Design System

To ship on time, we adopted an agile approach to tactically shipping pages that fed back into a dynamic, evolving design system.

Expansion

The test cases were such a success that the design system was rapidly adopted to upgrade the rest of Nasdaq.com
